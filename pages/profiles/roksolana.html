Dear younger me_

Women leaders give advice to their younger selves.

Roksolana Parygin

Director of Email Marketing at Inflection

Share Roksolana's insights:

Roksolana, what has made you so fabulous?

I work very hard and laugh even harder every day.

How did you become passionate about technology?

I experienced how quickly my work was able to make an impact on my team and the bottom line.

What is a myth that you have learned to be untrue?

I used to think that I needed to be brash and overpowering to be taken seriously or be successful. That was a myth. Aligning a team to work towards a common goal with empathy and expectation of hard work is a much greater measure of professional success.

What is the best part of being a successful woman in 2015?

I am making a dent in leveling the playing field. I have the privilege of working with the next generation of successful women.
