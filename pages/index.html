About Dear Younger Me

The idea of reflecting on our professional journeys and sharing encouragement with our "younger selves" came from discussions amongst several of the women leaders at inflection. We wanted to share what we've learned and the amazing experiences we've crearted for ourselves in the technology industry. We want every "younger me" to feel empowered to join and innovate this space.
